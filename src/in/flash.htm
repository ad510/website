I programmed these Flash games, and my brother Matthew made the art. Please give them a high rating if you like them!

Momentum Master: Momentum Master is a challenging physics-based swinging game with a Skill Mode and Puzzle & Challenge Levels. The game is also on Kongregate.
Momentum Master II and the World Wide Web: Swing your way around the world in this challenging physics-based game with a Skill Mode and 26 levels. The game is also on Kongregate.
Myrrored: Myrrored is a puzzle game that challenges you to manipulate mirrors to redirect lasers into laser sensors. The game is also on the iOS App Store.
